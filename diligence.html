Review architecture

Six connected passes through the benefits workstream.

Each pass creates a structured output and feeds the next one. A finding is not treated as final until its evidence and status are clear.

1

Intake & classify

Inventory files, identify entities and plan years, classify document types, and flag unreadable or missing materials.

2

Build the program map

Map medical/Rx, dental, vision, life, disability, HSA/FSA, COBRA, EAP, and administration relationships.

3

Analyze design & cost

Capture plan design, rates, premiums, contributions, enrollment, and financial inconsistencies.

4

Screen compliance & structure

Review plan documents, Form 5500 indicators, COBRA, Section 125, funding, portability, and transaction-specific questions.

5

Resolve open evidence

Connect DRL requests, seller responses, call notes, and new uploads back to the original issue and source record.

6

Produce decision outputs

Generate workpapers, exhibits, issue summaries, audience-specific report sections, and post-close actions.
