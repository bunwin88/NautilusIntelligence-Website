Request a Free Fit Review.

The Fit Review is a structured first conversation to determine whether Nautilus Diligence fits the transaction, what evidence is available, and which first deliverable would be useful.

What to send

Start with metadata and scope.

1

Transaction contextStock, asset, carve-out, add-on, platform, or exploratory review.

2

Review objectiveThe decision, risk, workflow, or deliverable you need to support.

3

Available materialsDocument categories, plan years, buyer/target status, and approximate file volume.

4

Timing and audienceDeadline, reporting cadence, and who will use the output.

5

Current frictionManual steps, missing visibility, inconsistent workpapers, or follow-up problems.

What you receive

A practical fit recommendation.

  • Recommended first review path
  • Proposed input and output scope
  • Known limitations or evidence gaps
  • Suggested acceptance criteria
  • Next-step plan for a pilot or managed review
Do not send in the first email: employee-level data, Social Security numbers, claims, medical information, payroll files, confidential deal documents, or full data-room exports.
